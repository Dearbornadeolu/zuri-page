scope of program

Here is the list of areas that will be explored in the training.

Frontend Web Development

This track deals with the aspect of your application that the user interact with. A front-end developer will be able to correctly interpret a given design to the user interface.

Backend Web Development

This track deals with the aspect of your application that the user does not directly interact with. its allows the front-end function either by running some Backend code or connecting to the database.

Product Design

This track deals with creating a graphics plan for an appllication you will learn to convert project documentation introducea viewable and understandable graphic design for developers to work with.

DevOPS

This track is an extention of the backend track. it involves tools that will allow the participant to deploy solutionfor public use.

Fullstack Development

A combination of frontend, backend, and Devops. This will only be available to participants who have the time to dedicate to the training.

Frameworks

This track deals with the aspect of your application that the user interact with. A front-end developer will be able to correctly interpret a given design to the user interface.

Got Questions ?

Frequently Asked Questions

Would i have to pay to enroll for the Training?

No, the program is completely free thanks to INGRESSIVE For Good

What are the things i would need to participate

You will need a laptop, internet connection And the willingness to learn through collaboration and mentorship.

What happens if i join and i'm not active?

participate who join the program and stop engaging will be given a time to catch up. You'll also be assigned a 'study-mate'. and the mentor to help you. However, there is the possibility of not being promoted to the next stage if you remain inactive for too long.

Can i switch from part-time to full time? +

Unfortunately, no, you cannot switch from part-time to full-time and vice-versa.

Will i get payed?

We reward some participant who complete tasks early, however, this is not a weekly reward all work that would be done during the program are hypothetical

Structure

Program Structure

There are 2 broad paths in this program based on your availability

Full Time

Requires at least 25 hours weekly on program content

Available Tracks

  • Full stack: Backend (python, javascript), Frontend(HTML,CSS,JAVASCRIPT)& DevOps
  • Design: Figma, Adobe XD

Phases:

  • Training phase
  • Project Phase

Post Programs

  • Certificate of Attendance
  • zuri Internship

    Zuri Internship Is Optional

    What do we mean by this?
    The Zuri Internship is another level of training that places the participants in a work simulation. It is not paid work; it’s a short training program to get participants ready for actual jobs. There will be cash rewards for a job well done.

  • Laptop Reward (upgrade or new one)

    Laptop Reward (Upgrade Or New One)

    What do we mean by this? We will be working with our partners on how to get laptops for exceptional participants who genuinely need a better device. This is not for everyone, and we will only do this if we can get partners to fund / or donate.

  • job placement

    Job Reward:

    This is a beginner-focused program; we will not be exclusively recommending everyone for jobs or job placement. We will not be placing everyone in our talent pool. We will select very few who are exceptional, smart and able to out-perform their pairs and recommend them for possible employment or further internships. You might still have to go through other forms of interview depending on the employer

  • Zuri Talent Pool
  • More traning :

    More Training

    What do we mean by this? We will be partnering with more companies to provide more training to participants in this track. However, this is subject to the partners. If we cannot get partners in any of the items listed below, we will not be doing the training

  • Interview Prep
  • Algorithms & Data
  • Structure & Data
  • Freelance SetUp & Training
  • LinkedIn Optimization
  • CV Review

Part Time

Requires at least 11 hours weekly content.

Available Tracks

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT
  • Backend: Can only select one backend language
    PHYTHON, PHP, JAVASCRIPT
  • Design: Figma
